Smoking Cessation Program

The best time to quit smoking was yesterday. The next best time is today — and a structured, personalised program can make quitting safer and far more successful.

Understanding smoking cessation

Quit safely. Quit for good.

Every cigarette affects your lungs, heart, blood vessels and overall health. Yet many smokers continue, believing the damage is already done or that they can stop whenever they want. The reality is different.

Regardless of how long you have smoked, quitting can significantly improve your lung function, reduce disease risk and improve your quality of life. The body begins repairing itself soon after smoking stops.

At Healthy Chest & Sleep Clinic, Dr. Vishal Sharma helps smokers quit safely and successfully through structured smoking cessation programs designed to reduce cravings, manage withdrawal symptoms and support long-term success.

Our approach

How we help you quit.

Every smoker is different. We build a strategy around your smoking pattern, lifestyle and readiness to quit.

Understanding your smoking pattern

We evaluate smoking history, daily consumption, previous quit attempts, triggers and nicotine dependence level.

Personalised quit plan

A customised strategy is developed based on your lifestyle and readiness to quit.

Managing withdrawal symptoms

You are guided through the challenging early phase with practical strategies and medical support where appropriate.

Lung health assessment

Many smokers benefit from evaluating current lung health and identifying early signs of smoking-related disease.

Ongoing support & follow-up

Regular follow-up helps improve accountability, manage setbacks and increase long-term success rates.
