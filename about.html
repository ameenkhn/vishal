Meet Dr. Vishal Sharma

Internationally trained.
Locally devoted.

Dr. Vishal Sharma is a consultant pulmonologist and sleep specialist based in Chandigarh. After completing his MRCP from the Royal College of Physicians (UK) and his European Diploma in Respiratory Medicine (EDRM, Switzerland), he returned to India with a clear mission: deliver respiratory and sleep care that meets the highest international benchmarks while remaining accessible to families in his home region.

His practice combines deep clinical expertise with a quiet, attentive bedside manner — patients consistently describe their consultations as the most thorough they've ever had.

Our mission

Care defined by clarity, evidence and dignity.

We believe respiratory and sleep medicine should be guided by what's actually wrong — not by what's trending or convenient. Every recommendation we make is grounded in current international guidelines, your clinical picture, and what genuinely matters to you.

  • Evidence-basedTreatments aligned with the latest GINA, GOLD & AASM guidance.
  • TransparentClear costs, plain-language explanations, no unnecessary tests.
  • ContinuousStructured follow-up so your plan keeps working long term.
What we stand for

Three pillars of our practice.

01

Listen first, test second

Most diagnoses are made from a careful history. Investigations should narrow the picture — not replace the conversation.

02

Right care, right time

We escalate when needed and step down when appropriate. Your plan adapts as your symptoms change.

03

Treat the person, not the report

Lifestyle, work, family — all of it shapes a good plan. We design treatment around your life, not the other way around.

Inside the clinic

A calm, modern space — designed for accurate diagnosis.

Our clinic in NAC Manimajra is purpose-built around the patient experience. Quiet consultation rooms, an in-house pulmonary function lab, dedicated nebuliser bay and a private sleep-study suite mean most of your assessments happen under one roof.

  • In-house diagnosticsSpirometry, FeNO, peak flow, six-minute walk test, ECG.
  • Sleep labType-1 polysomnography & home sleep tests with full reporting.
  • Modern technologyLatest-generation diagnostic equipment with strict QC protocols.
