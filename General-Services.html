Sleep Test Level I / II (Polysomnography — Lab-Based)

A comprehensive, supervised overnight study that measures how well your lungs inhale, exhale and transfer oxygen. Helps diagnose obstructive sleep apnoea, COPD-related sleep issues, REM disorders and complex sleep-disordered breathing with full specialist reporting.

  • Full EEG, ECG, EMG, EOG & respiratory channels
  • Technician-attended through the night
  • Specialist-graded report within 48 hours
  • CPAP / BiPAP titration & ongoing review

Sleep Test Level III (Home Sleep Study)

A convenient home-based sleep screening test. Ideal for suspected obstructive sleep apnoea in patients without complex comorbidities — monitoring oxygen, breathing patterns, heart rate and snoring from the comfort of your own home.

  • Device set up with full instructions
  • Non-invasive, lightweight sensors
  • Specialist-interpreted report
  • Seamless escalation to lab test if needed
Book a home study

Diagnostics

Pulmonary Function Test (PFT)

A comprehensive breathing test that measures how well your lungs inhale, exhale and transfer oxygen. Essential for diagnosing and monitoring asthma, COPD, ILD and other respiratory conditions. Simple, non-invasive, and provides instant results for clinical decision-making.

  • Spirometry with bronchodilator reversibility
  • Lung volumes & diffusion capacity (DLCO)
  • Fractional exhaled nitric oxide (FeNO)
  • Six-minute walk test & pulse oximetry
Book a PFT
Gas-transfer assessment
Diagnostics

DLCO (Diffusing Capacity of the Lungs for Carbon Monoxide)

DLCO measures how effectively your lungs transfer oxygen into the blood. It is essential for evaluating lung fibrosis, interstitial lung disease, pulmonary hypertension and long-standing asthma or COPD — helping guide accurate treatment decisions.

  • Single-breath technique, takes ~15 minutes
  • Useful for ILD, emphysema & PAH work-up
  • Specialist-interpreted, clear plain-language report
Book a DLCO test

Bronchoscopy

A minimally invasive procedure that uses a thin camera to look inside your airways. Used to diagnose infections, tumours, bleeding and unexplained cough, and to collect samples for biopsy or culture — performed under conscious sedation in a day-care setting.

  • Flexible video bronchoscopy
  • Bronchoalveolar lavage & biopsy
  • Foreign body retrieval where required
  • Same-day discharge in most cases

Thoracoscopy

A keyhole procedure that allows direct visualisation of the pleural cavity (lining of the lungs). Used to diagnose and treat pleural effusion, suspected pleural cancer, infections and unexplained fluid accumulation — with biopsies taken under direct vision for accuracy.

  • Diagnostic medical thoracoscopy
  • Pleural biopsy & fluid drainage
  • Talc pleurodesis where indicated
  • Performed under conscious sedation

Endobronchial Ultrasound (EBUS) / FNAC / Biopsy

An advanced ultrasound-assisted bronchoscopy used for diagnosing lung cancer, lymph-node enlargement, tuberculosis and mediastinal disease. Allows precise needle aspiration or biopsy with minimal discomfort and high diagnostic yield.

  • EBUS-guided needle aspiration (TBNA)
  • FNAC for lung & mediastinal lesions
  • Targeted biopsy for accurate diagnosis
  • Faster, safer than traditional methods

Diagnostics

Forced Oscillation Technique (FOT)

A modern, effortless lung-function test done during normal breathing — ideal for children, the elderly and adults who cannot perform routine PFT. It measures airway resistance and reactance to detect early lung disease, often before symptoms appear.

  • No forced manoeuvres required
  • Ideal for paediatric & elderly patients
  • Detects small-airway disease early
  • Quick — completes in just a few minutes
Book a FOT test

Smoking Cessation

Quitting smoking is the single most powerful thing you can do for your lungs. We pair medication where appropriate with behavioural support and short, supportive follow-ups to keep you on track.

  • Personalised quit plan & trigger mapping
  • NRT & varenicline where appropriate
  • Weekly check-ins for the first month

Preventive

Adult Vaccination

Influenza, pneumococcal and COVID vaccinations are particularly important if you live with chronic lung disease, are over 60, or care for someone vulnerable. We can also advise on travel-related vaccines.

  • Pneumococcal (PCV / PPSV)
  • COVID boosters
