About the Clinic

Dedicated specialist care for lung & sleep health.

At Healthy Chest & Sleep Clinic we focus exclusively on the diagnosis and management of complex respiratory diseases and sleep disorders. Our practice combines evidence-based medicine with advanced diagnostics and individualised treatment — designed to deliver measurable, long-term outcomes.

  • Specialist-led consultationsEvery plan is reviewed and signed off by a consultant.
  • Full pulmonary & sleep diagnosticsSpirometry, polysomnography, FeNO, bronchoscopy & more.
  • Transparent, ethical practiceTests are recommended only when clinically necessary.

How it works

Your path to clearer lungs & better sleep.

A clear, four-step pathway that respects your time and keeps you informed at every stage.

01

Book an Appointment

Schedule online or by phone — same-week slots available, with telephone triage if you need urgent advice.

02

Specialist Consultation

A focused, unhurried consultation with Dr. Sharma — listening first, then a clinical examination and clear next steps.
