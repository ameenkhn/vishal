About the Clinic

Dedicated specialist care for lung & sleep health.

At Healthy Chest & Sleep Clinic we focus exclusively on the diagnosis and management of complex respiratory diseases and sleep disorders. Our practice combines evidence-based medicine with advanced diagnostics and individualised treatment — designed to deliver measurable, long-term outcomes.

  • Specialist-led consultationsEvery plan is reviewed and signed off by a consultant.
  • Full pulmonary & sleep diagnosticsSpirometry, polysomnography, FeNO, bronchoscopy & more.
  • Transparent, ethical practiceTests are recommended only when clinically necessary.

Services we offer

Diagnostic procedures & interventions.

A complete in-house suite of pulmonary diagnostics and interventional procedures — so most of your assessment and treatment happens under one roof.

PFT Lab

Complete lung-function assessment — Spirometry (PFT), FeNO, DLCO (diffusion capacity) and FOT (Forced Oscillation Technique).

Learn more

Sleep Studies

Level I/II in-lab polysomnography and Level III home sleep tests for OSA, snoring and other sleep disorders.

Learn more

Bronchoscopy

Flexible & rigid bronchoscopy with BAL, TBNA, biopsy and foreign-body removal.

Learn more

Thoracoscopy

Medical pleuroscopy for diagnosis & treatment of pleural disease.

Learn more

Endobronchial Ultrasound

Radial & convex-probe EBUS for mediastinal & lung lesions.

Learn more

Allergy Testing

Skin-prick & specific-IgE testing for respiratory allergy.

Learn more

Smoking Cessation Clinic

Structured programmes & pharmacological support to help you quit for good.

Learn more

Vaccinations

Influenza, pneumococcal & other adult respiratory vaccinations.

How it works

Your path to clearer lungs & better sleep.

A clear, four-step pathway that respects your time and keeps you informed at every stage.

01

Book an Appointment

Schedule online or by phone — same-week slots available, with telephone triage if you need urgent advice.

02

Specialist Consultation

A focused, unhurried consultation with Dr. Sharma — listening first, then a clinical examination and clear next steps.

03

Tailored Diagnostics

Only the tests you genuinely need — spirometry, FeNO, sleep study, HRCT or bronchoscopy — performed in-house where possible.

An evidence-based plan in plain language, with structured follow-up and accessible support between visits.
