Allergy & Respiratory Allergy Treatment

Frequent sneezing, a blocked or runny nose, throat irritation or breathing problems that worsen with weather changes may be signs of an underlying respiratory allergy that needs proper care.

Understanding respiratory allergy

Identify triggers. Breathe freely again.

A respiratory allergy occurs when the immune system reacts excessively to substances that are normally harmless. These substances, known as allergens, can trigger inflammation in the nose, throat, airways and lungs.

While many people dismiss recurrent sneezing, cough or breathing problems as "seasonal issues," they may actually be signs of an underlying respiratory allergy. Left unmanaged, these can contribute to conditions such as asthma, chronic cough and recurrent respiratory infections.

At Healthy Chest & Sleep Clinic, Dr. Vishal Sharma provides comprehensive evaluation and treatment for respiratory allergies, helping patients identify triggers and achieve long-term symptom control.
